The only way to access 1687 is to hold one of the limited edition 1687 unique digital NFTs. The 1687 NFT serves as the owner’s digital identity and membership card, and provides access to exclusive members-only benefits, like limited edition products from the Cannavallé vault, members-only NFT drops, special events, private forums, and more.

The Specs

Each 1687 Cat is unique and programmatically generated from over 170 possible traits, including expression, headwear, clothing, and more. All cat are dope, but some are rarer than others.

The cats are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS. (See Record and Proof.) Purchasing a cat costs 0.08 ETH.

To access members-only events and places, Catholders will need to be signed into their Metamask Wallet
